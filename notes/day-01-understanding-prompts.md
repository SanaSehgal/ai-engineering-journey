## My Goal

I want to understand what prompt engineering actually is and why it matters when building AI applications.

Instead of memorizing concepts, I want to learn by running small experiments and documenting what I discover.

## What is a Prompt?

A prompt is the instruction or information I give to an AI model.

The quality of the prompt can influence the quality of the response.

A good prompt reduces ambiguity by clearly describing what I want.
